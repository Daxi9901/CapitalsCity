About GeoQuiz

GeoQuiz is a free geography learning tool designed to help players learn and memorize the capitals of all countries of the world. The goal of the site is simple: make learning geography fun, fast, and interactive.

The website contains multiple quiz modes, including Europe, Asia, Africa, North America, South America, Oceania, and a full World quiz. Each mode includes a timer, score counter, and instant answer checking to help train memory and improve reaction time.

GeoQuiz is a completely free and independent project created with the intention to help students, travelers, and geography lovers test their knowledge.

No account or login is required to use GeoQuiz. All quizzes run directly in your browser and do not store personal information.

If you have suggestions, bug reports, or new ideas, feel free to contact me.
